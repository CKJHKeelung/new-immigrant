Filipino Language Lesson

Unit Theme: Surviving in the Philippines (About Food) 單元主題：Surviving in the Philippines (關於食物與日常)

This unit covers essential vocabulary and practical phrases for everyday life in the Philippines. Students will learn how to navigate public transportation, order food at restaurants, shop at local markets, and confidently explore the streets. 本單元涵蓋菲律賓日常生活中的核心詞彙與實用短語。學生將學習如何辨識、搭乘當地的公共交通工具、在餐廳點餐、在當地市場購物，並充滿自信地探索菲律賓的街道。

School:承辦學校： Chenggong JH成功國中
Designer: John Marvin Raganas
Grade Level: Grade 8
Duration: 45 mins (10:10-10:55)

Unit Overview: Rationale & Philosophy 設計理念與單元概述

"Surviving in the Philippines covers essential vocabulary and practical phrases for everyday life. By equipping Grade 8 learners with realistic scenarios of navigation, ordering food, and local market shopping, they build the confidence to wander, communicate, and solve real-life challenges." 「"Surviving in the Philippines" 專注於日常生活必備單字與實用短語。藉由教導八年級學生如何問路指路、餐廳點餐及當地市場採購等真實情境，幫助學生建立探索世界的信心、提升日常溝通能力，並能自主解決生活中的各種挑戰。」

Learning Objectives 學習目標

  • a Identify basic vocabulary for common modes of transportation in the Philippines. 認讀與指認菲律賓常見公共交通工具的基本字彙。
  • b Recognize familiar Filipino words for various places and destinations. 辨識日常生活中常見地點與目的地的菲律賓語單字。
  • c Use essential conversational phrases to ask for and give simple commuting directions. 熟練運用核心溝通語句進行問路與交通路線引導。
  • d Actively participate in interactive language drills and communicative games. 積極參與課堂語言演練與通勤互動遊戲（賓果/雙輪盤）。

Preliminaries課前問候

Greeting & Small Talk 師生相見歡與日常問答

  • Greet the students: "Magandang Umaga!" 與學生互道早安："Magandang Umaga!" (早安！)
  • Ask how they are: "Kumusta ka?" 詢問近況與心情："Kumusta ka?" (你好嗎？)
  • Ask about the weather: "Kumusta ang panahon ngayon?" 討論今天的天氣狀況："Kumusta ang panahon ngayon?" (今天天氣如何？)
5 mins Interactive board presentation Students respond appropriately. 學生能以對應語境做出正確的回應與對話。
Preparation課前引導

Transportation Guess & Encircle 菲律賓特色通勤載具大猜謎

  • Show different pictures of common transportation means. 大螢幕秀出菲律賓各種常見交通工具與街頭風情照片。
  • Let students guess and encircle which are the most common transportation in the Philippines. 邀約學生上前上台圈選出他們認為在當地最普及的交通形式。
- Local transportation pictures Students participate by encircling the pictures. 學生熱烈舉手並主動上台圈選。
Presentation核心授課

Core Vocabulary & Phraseology 本課重點字彙與會話教授

[PART 1: TRANSPORTATION VOCABULARY & TRIVIA]

Discussion: Introduce and discuss the most common modes of transportation in the Philippines. 互動探討：介紹並深度探索菲律賓生活最不可或缺的通勤文化。

Vocabulary: Introduce the vehicles: Taksi (Taxi), Traysikel (Tricycle), Bus, Habal-habal (Motorcycle taxi), and Jeep/Dyip (Jeepney). 生字解析：講授五大核心載具 Taksi (計程車)、Traysikel (三輪車)、Bus (巴士)、Habal-habal (摩托計程車) 及 Dyip/Jeepney (吉普尼)。

Pronunciation Drill: Have the students repeat and practice pronouncing each word aloud. 拼讀演練：師生全班齊讀，修正並加強單字重音位置。

[PART 2: PLACES REVIEW]

Briefly review common locations and landmarks, asking students to recall or guess their Filipino translations. 快速複習生活中的八大常用景點名稱，邀請學生拼出對應的菲律賓語。

[PART 3: COMMUTING PHRASES]

Show contextual pictures and let students guess the correct phrases. Introduce daily commute phrases:
a. Paano pumunta sa...?
b. Sumakay ka ng...
c. Paabot po.
d. Para po! 播放情境圖引導同學猜測應答短語。帶入四大基本句型：
a. Paano pumunta sa...? (如何去...？)
b. Sumakay ka ng... (搭乘...)
c. Paabot po. (請幫忙傳遞車資)
d. Para po! (停車請在此下車)

15 mins Interactive board presentation Students participate and recite. 考察學生在課堂中的複誦流暢度與理解反應。
Practice雙輪盤演練

Double Roulette Speaking Drill 對稱式雙輪盤即興造句

  • Show two dynamic roulettes on screen. 投影雙輪盤，隨機邀請班上同學上台點擊轉盤。
  • Call random students to roll and practice the combined sentence structures. 根據隨機轉出的結果套入公式，練習回答：地點與對應建議交通方式。
5 mins Interactive Board Roulette Oral participation and syntax accuracy. 學生口語發言積極度及應對速度。
Production小組實踐

Commute Bingo Challenge 全員大走動：通勤大賓果競賽

  • Distribute Bingo cards (8 destination slots) and role cards. 發給全體同學個人 8 格賓果卡與身分交通工具角色卡。
  • Instruct students to mingle and find classmates with matching destinations using Filipino phrases. 學生在座位間走動，與同學展開菲語問答 "Paano pumunta sa [地點]?"。
  • The respondent replies "Sumakay ka ng [vehicle]" to let the inquirer write down the answer. 被問者依據自己手牌回答 "Sumakay ka ng [交通工具]"。
  • First to complete the line shouts "Para po!" to be crowned the winner. 最快集滿拼圖連成線的人大喊 "Para po!" 宣告贏家。
15 mins Printed Bingo worksheets
Hand markers 		Observational speech evaluation; grammar correct use. 觀察學生對話過程中是否主動運用正確句型交流。
Wrap-Up課堂統整

Cultural Reflection & Extension 跨文化比較與下課反思

  • What is one thing you find interesting about Filipino transportation? 探討：你覺得菲律賓最令人印象深刻或有趣的交通工具是什麼？為什麼？
  • What is one thing you think you want to bring to Taiwan? 思考：若可以，你會想引進什麼交通工具到台灣？
5 mins Interactive Board Slide Evaluate students' reflections and active participation. 評估學生發表與對課堂教學回饋的反思深度。
